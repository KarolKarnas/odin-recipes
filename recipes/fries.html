Fries

Description

My fries are the best because they are made by beginner coder

Mmm... delicious! :)

Ingredients

  • Potatoes 500g
  • Sunflower oil 2l
  • Salt 20g
  • Mayo 200ml
  • Ketchup 200ml

Mmm... delicious! :)

Steps

  1. Peel the Potatoes
  2. Warm up the oil
  3. Cut the Potatoes to 8mm fries
  4. Fry 15 minutes in 170C
  5. Add salt, mayo, ketchup
  6. Enjoy your meal
